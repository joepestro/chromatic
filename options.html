Chromatic

Chromatic responds to your voice commands

  • Control Chrome Open, close, scroll, go back & forward

    "Scroll down slowly"
    "Browse to Facebook"
    "Go back a page"

  • Search the web Google, YouTube, Twitter, + many more

    "Search google for a chicken recipe"
    "Find a cute dog video"
    "Show tweets that mention earthquake"

  • Check Gmail You'll need to sign in to Gmail first

    "How many emails do I have?"
    "Read new messages to me"
    "Send an email to Joe"

  • Set Reminders Triggers a notification with your message

    "Remind me to pick up milk"
    "Remind me to take a break in 30 minutes"
    "Remind me to exercise at 4:30"

  • Tune Grooveshark Will open Grooveshark if not already open

    "Play some music"
    "Next song"
    "Turn down the volume"

  • Get answers Powered by DuckDuckGo & Wolfram|Alpha

    "Calculate 2+2+2+5+7"
    "What is a turing machine?"
    "What is the capital of Ohio?"
